Support

If you need help with Beat the Odds, contact us by email.

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Support email: purecodebase@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Device model and iOS version
  • App version
  • Short issue description
  • Screenshots or screen recording if possible

Purchases and Billing

  • Beat the Odds offers a one-time premium unlock.
  • The premium unlock is a non-consumable purchase and can be restored on the same Apple account.
  • If premium does not unlock after purchase, restart the app first and then use Restore Purchases in the Premium screen.
  • If you were charged but premium was not restored, email support with your receipt or order number.
  • Refunds are handled by Apple: Request a refund

Ads and Privacy

  • If you see an inappropriate or age-inappropriate ad, email purecodebase@gmail.com with the approximate time, your device, and a short description.
  • If possible, include a screenshot of the ad or the screen where it appeared.
  • Privacy choices can be reviewed in the app under Settings -> Review Privacy Choices.
  • Tracking permission can be reviewed in the app under Settings -> Tracking Permission.
  • A public summary of these controls is available here: Privacy Choices.

Gameplay and Save Data

  • Game progress, stats, and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • If the app is removed or app data is cleared, progress may be lost and may not be recoverable.
  • Ad availability can depend on region, device settings, consent state, or temporary inventory availability.
  • Premium is comfort-only and does not change odds, points, attempts, or gameplay outcomes.

Privacy and Legal Contact

  • For privacy requests, support issues, or legal contact, email purecodebase@gmail.com.
  • Provider details are available in the Impressum.
