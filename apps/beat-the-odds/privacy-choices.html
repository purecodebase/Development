Privacy Choices

For: Beat the Odds: Luck Run

Last updated: April 3, 2026

1. Review Consent Choices in the App

  • Open Settings -> Review Privacy Choices to reopen the consent flow when available in your region.
  • Consent availability depends on local law, platform rules, and the ad stack currently active in the app.

2. Tracking Permission on iPhone

  • Open Settings -> Tracking Permission inside the app to check the current App Tracking Transparency status.
  • If iOS has already shown the permission prompt, you may need to manage tracking access in the iPhone Settings app.

3. Local Save Data

  • Run progress, statistics, settings, tutorial state, and similar gameplay data are stored locally on your device.
  • If you remove the app or clear its local data, this gameplay data may be lost and may not be recoverable.

4. Purchases and Restore

  • Premium is a one-time non-consumable unlock purchased through Apple App Store billing.
  • If you already bought premium on the same Apple account, use Restore Purchases from the Premium screen.

5. Support and Privacy Requests

For privacy or support requests, email purecodebase@gmail.com.
