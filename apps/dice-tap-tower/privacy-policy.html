For: Dice Tap Tower

Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. Controller

This policy describes how PureCodeBase (owner: Nico Wuerthenberger) processes data in Dice Tap Tower.

2. Scope

This page applies only to the game Dice Tap Tower.

3. Data We Process

3.1 Local Game and Device Data

  • Local game progress, upgrades, rewards, and settings stored on your device
  • Device and app environment metadata required by platform and ad SDKs
  • Diagnostics and performance-related metadata generated by integrated SDKs

3.2 Ads and Tracking Related Data

  • Advertising identifiers and advertising data, subject to platform permission and consent state
  • Coarse location, product interaction, and ad impression or interaction data used for ad delivery and measurement
  • On iOS, App Tracking Transparency (ATT) status and related consent choices

3.3 Purchase Related Data

  • Transaction status and product fulfillment metadata for consumable purchases
  • No raw payment card data is processed by us

4. Third-Party Processors / SDKs

  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK (ad delivery and measurement)
  • Apple App Store and Google Play billing systems (purchase processing)

5. Legal Basis and Consent

  • Core game operation and local progress: legitimate interest / contract necessity
  • Ads, tracking, and related data processing: consent where required by law
  • On iOS, ATT is requested only when the personalized ads flow is relevant
  • Privacy choices can be reviewed in the app under Settings -> Review Privacy Choices

6. Data Retention

Game progression is primarily stored locally on your device. If the app is removed, local data may be lost. Third-party providers retain their data according to their own retention and compliance policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Third-party providers may process data outside your country. Provider safeguards and applicable legal transfer mechanisms apply where required.
