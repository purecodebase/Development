Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how PureCodeBase ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use Turnforge Legends.

This policy applies to:

  • Turnforge Legends - Turn-based RPG

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

The app does not require account creation with your real name or email. Depending on your region, permissions, and settings, certain identifiers and usage data may be processed for ads, analytics, purchases, and support.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the app, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics and diagnostics
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID), subject to platform permission and consent state

2.3 Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

Some of our apps display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed through Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. All transactions are handled securely by the respective platform.

Purchase entitlement/status metadata may be processed via RevenueCat.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our apps
  • Save your game progress locally on your device
  • Display advertisements (where applicable)
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

4. Data Storage

Your game progress, settings, and statistics are stored locally on your device using standard platform storage mechanisms. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

If you uninstall an app, all locally stored data for that app will be deleted permanently.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with:

  • Analytics providers to improve our services
  • Advertising partners (Google AdMob) for ad delivery

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at the email address below.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.

8. Advertising and Consent Preferences

Where required (for example EU/EEA/UK), ad-consent choices are provided in-app. On iOS, App Tracking Transparency (ATT) controls tracking permission.

You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • iOS: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Tracking ‚Üí Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

Declining tracking/personalized ads does not block core gameplay functionality.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
