Privacy Policy

For: Two Worlds Infinity

Last updated: March 5, 2026

1. Controller

This policy describes how PureCodeBase (owner: Nico Wuerthenberger) processes data in Two Worlds Infinity.

2. Scope

This page applies only to the game Two Worlds Infinity.

3. Data We Process

3.1 In-Game and Device Data

  • Gameplay and event telemetry (if analytics consent is enabled)
  • Device and app environment metadata required by SDKs
  • Crash diagnostics (if analytics/crash consent is enabled)
  • Local game progression state stored on device

3.2 Ads and Tracking Related Data

  • Advertising identifiers (for example IDFA/GAID), subject to consent and platform settings
  • Ad impression/interactions metadata via ad network SDKs

3.3 Purchase Related Data

  • Purchase entitlement and transaction status metadata through store providers and RevenueCat
  • No raw payment card data is processed by us

4. Third-Party Processors / SDKs

  • Google AdMob (ads delivery and measurement)
  • RevenueCat (purchase entitlement management)
  • Firebase Analytics (usage analytics, consent-gated)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (crash diagnostics, consent-gated)

5. Legal Basis and Consent

  • Core game operation: legitimate interest / contract necessity
  • Ads, analytics and tracking: consent where required by law
  • On iOS, App Tracking Transparency (ATT) is requested only for personalized ads flow
  • Consent choices can be changed in the in-app Privacy screen

6. Data Retention

Game progression is primarily stored locally on device. If the app is removed, local data may be lost. Third-party providers retain their data according to their own retention policies.

7. International Transfers

Third-party providers may process data outside your country. Standard contractual safeguards and provider compliance frameworks apply where required.

8. Your Rights

  • Access, correction, deletion and objection rights as applicable in your jurisdiction
  • Withdrawal of consent at any time (without retroactive effect)
