Privacy Policy

For: Beat the Odds

Last updated: April 3, 2026

1. Controller

This policy describes how PureCodeBase (owner: Nico Wuerthenberger) processes data in Beat the Odds.

2. Scope

This page applies only to the game Beat the Odds.

3. Data We Process

3.1 Local Game and Device Data

  • Local progress, statistics, medal/rank progress, settings, premium status, tutorial state, and ad pacing state stored on your device
  • Device and app environment metadata required by platform and SDK integrations
  • Diagnostics and technical metadata generated by integrated SDKs

3.2 Ads and Tracking Related Data

  • Advertising identifiers and related ad measurement data, subject to consent state and platform permissions
  • Ad impression, interaction, and coarse device or contextual data used for ad delivery and measurement
  • On iOS, App Tracking Transparency (ATT) status and privacy choices

3.3 Purchase Related Data

  • Purchase status, entitlement status, and transaction fulfillment metadata for the one-time premium unlock
  • Store account payment data is processed by Apple and RevenueCat, not by us directly

4. Third-Party Processors / SDKs

  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK (ad delivery and measurement)
  • Google User Messaging Platform (consent and privacy messaging)
  • RevenueCat (purchase and entitlement management)
  • Apple App Store billing systems (purchase processing)

5. Legal Basis and Consent

  • Core game operation and local progress: legitimate interest / contract necessity
  • Ads, tracking, and related data processing: consent where required by law
  • On iOS, ATT is requested only when the personalized ads flow is relevant
  • Privacy choices can be reviewed in the app under Settings -> Review Privacy Choices
  • Tracking permission status can be reviewed in the app under Settings -> Tracking Permission

6. Data Retention

Game progression is primarily stored locally on your device. If the app is removed, local data may be lost. Third-party providers retain their data according to their own retention and compliance policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Beat the Odds is not intentionally designed to collect personal information from children. If you believe inappropriate data handling has occurred, contact us using the address below.

8. International Transfers

Third-party providers may process data outside your country. Provider safeguards and applicable legal transfer mechanisms apply where required.

9. Your Rights

  • Access, correction, deletion, and objection rights as applicable in your jurisdiction
  • Withdrawal of consent at any time, without retroactive effect
  • Support requests via the email address listed below
