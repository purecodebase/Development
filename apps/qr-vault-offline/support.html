Support

If you need help with QR Vault Offline, contact us by email.

Support email: purecodebase@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Device model and OS version
  • App version
  • Short issue description
  • Screenshots or screen recording if possible

Premium Unlock and Billing

  • QR Vault Offline currently offers a one-time premium unlock.
  • Premium is restored through the same Apple account that completed the purchase.
  • If you were charged but premium did not unlock, restart the app first and then use Restore purchases in Settings.
  • Refunds are handled by Apple: Request a refund

Ads and Privacy

  • The free tier may show non-personalized ads.
  • If you see an inappropriate ad, email purecodebase@gmail.com with the approximate time, your device, and a short description.
  • Privacy choices can be reviewed in the app under Settings -> Ad privacy.

Local Data and Encryption

  • QR vault data is stored locally on your device.
  • If the app is removed or local app data is cleared, stored QR entries may be lost.
  • If encrypted storage is enabled and the underlying secure key material becomes unavailable, protected entries may no longer be readable.
