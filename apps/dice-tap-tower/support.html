Support

If you need help with Dice Tap Tower, contact us by email.

Support email: purecodebase@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Device model and OS version
  • App version
  • Short issue description
  • Screenshots or screen recording if possible

Purchases and Billing

  • All purchasable products in Dice Tap Tower are consumables.
  • Consumables are delivered immediately and cannot be restored after they have been used.
  • If you were charged but did not receive your items, restart the app first and then email support with your receipt or order number.
  • Refunds are handled by the platform where the purchase was made:
  • Apple: Request a refund
  • Google: Request a refund

Ads and Privacy

  • If you see an inappropriate or age-inappropriate ad, email purecodebase@gmail.com with the approximate time, your device, and a short description.
  • If possible, include a screenshot of the ad or the screen where it appeared.
  • Privacy choices can be reviewed in the app under Settings -> Review Privacy Choices.

Gameplay and Save Data

  • Game progress is stored locally on your device.
  • If the app is removed or app data is cleared, progress may be lost and may not be recoverable.
  • Ads require an internet connection and may depend on region, device settings, or temporary ad availability.
