Privacy Choices

For: Vibe Cards

Last updated: April 25, 2026

1. Review Consent Choices in the App

  • Open the Profile area of the app and use the privacy choices entry point when it is visible.
  • The entry point is shown only when the current Google consent flow requires it for your region or consent state.

2. Current Ad Setup

  • The current app version is designed around contextual AdMob banner placements on selected browse or social surfaces.
  • Create, preview, and certain utility flows are intended to remain ad-free in the current setup.
  • If the tracking-related ad setup changes in a future version, this page and the in-app privacy flow may be updated.

3. Public, Private, and Published Cards

  • You control whether a card stays private, becomes public, or is published into social surfaces such as Following or Discover.
  • You can remove a card from feed distribution by unpublishing it, or reduce its visibility by switching it back to private where supported.

4. Social Safety Controls

  • You can use block features to limit visibility between accounts where supported.
  • You can report public content that appears abusive, unlawful, or otherwise inappropriate.

5. Support and Privacy Requests

For privacy or support requests, email purecodebase@gmail.com.
